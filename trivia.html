  1. Which artist is most associated with the soft grunge aesthetic?

    The Arctic Monkeys, specifically, their album "AM" is most associated with the soft grunge aesthetic. The album cover's iconic waveform flooded Tumblr feeds, paired with reblogs of lyrics from popular songs "Do I Wanna Know?" and "R U Mine?". The Arctic Monkeys offered soft grunge teens an aspiration of a dark fantasy adulthood.

    Source: www.behindthecovers.com

  3. Which water bottle became the most iconic accessory of the VSCO girl aesthetic?

    The Hydro Flask was glued to every VSCO girl in 2019. It was often decorated with trendy stickers and a DIY hand-braided string keychain.

    Source: www.pinterest.com/melinaifti

  4. Which short-form video platform was popular in the early 2010's before shutting down in 2016?

    Vine was the most popular video-based app. It's signature feature was it's six-second limit on videos which pushed users to be fast, funny, and innovative. The platform became a central hub for meme culture, and many iconic Vines stayed relevant long after the app shut down in 2016.

    Source: www.youtube.com/warenya

  5. Lorde was only how old when "Royals" topped the charts in 2013?

    Lorde was only 16 when she released her debut album "Pure Heorine" in 2013. Her song "Royals" spent nine weeks as the top song on the Billboard Top 100. The song won Lorde a Grammy the following year for Best Pop Solo Performance.

    Source: www.metro.co.uk

  8. Which 2011 film starred Ryan Gosling as a stunt driver who moonlights as a getaway driver?

    Source: www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drive

  9. What flavor of frappuccino, released in 2017, caused widespread controversy for its taste?

    Starbucks released the limited-time Unicorn Frappuccino for one week in April 2017. The drink was highly controversial, with Katy Perry posting a video on her Instagram story of her trying it and spitting in out. Nonetheless, the drink's aesthetic visuals and limited-time availibility created so much demand for this drink that it went down in Starbucks history. In April 2025, Starbucks re-released the Unicorn Frappuccino exclusively for Coachella.

    Source: www.mashed.com

    Source: www.tumblr.com/tates-getaway
